#                                                            Mentora-Ai
#                                                 The ultimate academic cheat code!
Just imagine that you're staring at a mountain of unread lecture notes, a looming exam roadmap you haven't started, and a blank resume document. The friction between surviving this semester and actually preparing for your future feels overwhelming.We built Mentora AI because we believe no student should have to fight that battle alone.Mentora AI is your digital academic co-pilot. By automating the tedious parts of college—like summarizing dense PDFs and tracking attendance—it clears the mental clutter so you can focus on what matters. More importantly, it bridges the gap between the classroom and the corporate world, refining your skills and prepping you for the workforce long before graduation day.Turn the daily hustle into future fuel.
Mentora AI is an app mainly built for students struggling due to exam pressures,time management,understanding of notes and the exact preparation necessary to crack interviews of major companies.
It is an all-in-one, AI-powered academic and career companion designed for students. The core mission of the app is to help students with workflows, optimize study habits, and make them fully skill-efficient and career-ready. 
The key features of the app are : 
1. Notes Summarizer - You can drop the pdf of ur notes and the output will be fully summarized notes in an understandable manner along with some mindmaps and flowcharts.Best for the students having large syllabus but less time to cover it.
2. Assignment Planner - Helps plan your assignments as per the deadline and marks . It breaks down all the tasks efficiently and manages study time.
Exam Roadmaps - Provides personalized study paths generated by AI based on your syllabus, notes,extracurricular activities and exam dates. 
3. Smart Resume Builder -  Tailor your resume dynamically to highlight the skills you've gained during your courses. 
4. Interview Coach - Practice mock interviews with real-time feedback on your answers, tone, and pacing. 
The app is free for all the users worldwide and also has an option to upgrade the experience with our premium membership.
The app overall provides an exclusive study experience at almost no cost.
